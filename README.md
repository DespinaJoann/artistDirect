## About the Project
ArtistDirect is a digital art marketplace prototype developed as part of the compulsory academic assignment for the course "Information Systems & Electronic Business" at Harokopio University of Athens. The project focuses on designing and developing an e-business platform that connects independent artists with users through a digital environment where creators can present, promote and distribute their work. The main idea behind ArtistDirect is to support independent creators by providing a platform that promotes visibility, direct communication with audiences, transparency and respect for artistic ownership. Beyond the commercial aspect, ArtistDirect incorporates a social contribution perspective, aiming to connect artistic activity with positive social impact. The platform explores how digital commerce can support not only creators and users, but also broader social initiatives through a more responsible and human-centered approach. A key principle of ArtistDirect is the recognition and promotion of authentic human creativity. A key principle of ArtistDirect is the recognition and promotion of authentic human creativity. The platform is designed to showcase human-created artistic works and does not include AI-generated or AI-transformed content. By maintaining transparency regarding the origin of artistic creations, ArtistDirect supports a creator-focused environment where human expression remains at the center of the digital experience.

## Project Purpose
The purpose of this project was to transform an e-business idea into a functional digital prototype while exploring the relationship between technology, electronic commerce and user experience.
The platform was designed with emphasis on the role of technology as a tool that supports human creativity and enables new ways of interaction between creators and audiences.

## Development
Although the assignment suggested the use of Wix as a development environment, ArtistDirect was implemented from scratch using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
This approach allowed full control over the design, structure and user experience of the platform.
The current version represents a functional prototype that demonstrates the concept, interface and main direction of the future platform.

## Technologies
The project was developed using:
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
 
The prototype also integrates the Metropolitan Museum of Art Collection API to retrieve publicly available artwork information and display realistic content inside the platform.

## Current Prototype
The prototype includes the main sections of the ArtistDirect platform, such as:

- Homepage presentation
- Artwork marketplace
- Artist profiles
- Social impact section
- Artist registration concept

The current implementation uses prototype data and focuses on presenting the functionality and overall experience of the future e-business.

## Future Development
Future versions of ArtistDirect could include full-stack functionality such as user accounts, authentication, payments, order management, creator verification, analytics and additional mechanisms for protecting digital content and intellectual property.
